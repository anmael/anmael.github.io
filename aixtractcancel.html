How to Request Cancellation of your AIXtract Account

Please follow the steps below to request the cancellation of your AIXtract app account. This is a temporary measure, and in the future, you will be able to cancel directly from the app.

Step 1: Open your preferred email client or service.
Step 2: Click on the option to compose a new email.
Step 3: In the "To" field, enter the following email address: anklagonaow@gmail.com
Step 4: In the "Subject" field, you can enter something like "AIXtract Account Cancellation Request".
Step 5: In the body of the email, write the following sentence: "I request the deletion of my account."
Step 6: Once you've double-checked that all the information is correct, click the "Send" button.

After we receive your request, we will process the cancellation as soon as possible. We appreciate your patience during this process.
