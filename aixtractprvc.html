Privacy Policy for AIXtract

Effective Date: July 18, 2023

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and share your personal information when you use the AIXtract app.

Personal Information We Collect

The only personal information we collect is your gmail address, which is used for Firebase authentication. We do not collect any other personal information, such as your name, phone number, or location.

How We Use Your Personal Information

We use your personal information to:

  • Provide you with access to the AIXtract app
  • Authenticate your account
  • Improve the app

How We Share Your Personal Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties, except as follows:

  • We may share your personal information with our third-party service providers who help us operate the AIXtract app, such as Google Analytics. These service providers are required to keep your personal information confidential.
  • We may share your personal information if we are required to do so by law or if we believe that sharing is necessary to protect our rights or the rights of others.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make any material changes, we will notify you by email (sent to the email address specified in your account) or by means of a notice on this site prior to the change becoming effective.
